The problem

Malik counter pe nahi hota — paisa phir bhi leak hota hai

Lahore ki medical stores par POS chal raha hota hai, lekin owner ko asal dard yeh hai: cash short pata nahi kab hua, udhaar recover nahi ho raha, expiry pe maal zaya, supplier bill due aur staff pe shak.

💸

Cash short & chori

Shift close pe register aur system match nahi. Owner ko din ke end pe pata chalta hai — jab nuqsan ho chuka hota hai.

📦

Expiry & stock blind spot

POS mein alert hai lekin owner action nahi leta. Har mahine lakhs ka maal expire ho sakta hai.

📒

Udhaar & suppliers

Khata alag, POS alag. Kon recover karna hai aur is hafte kitna supplier ko dena hai — clear picture nahi.
